# premium-dog-outlet-tableau-dashboard
Data visualization project using Tableau to analyze sales, revenue trends, and business insights for Premium Dog Outlet.

## Project Overview
The Premium Dog Outlet Dashboard is a comprehensive Tableau-based business intelligence solution designed to analyze sales performance, branch distribution, and workforce metrics. The dashboard integrates employee data with sales insights to provide a complete view of operational and financial performance.

This project demonstrates advanced data visualization, KPI monitoring, and business storytelling using Tableau.

---

## Objective
To evaluate overall business performance by analyzing:

- Sales trends over time
- Category-wise sales distribution
- State-wise branch presence
- Branch-level employee distribution
- Workforce and product performance metrics

---

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Employees: 516  
- Total Branches: 20  
- Total Managers: 496  
- Total Products Sold: 567,298  

These KPIs provide a high-level summary of business scale and operational capacity.

---

## Dashboard Components

### 1. Category-Based Sales
Bar chart visualizing sales contribution across product categories such as:
- Dog Food
- Dog Accessories
- Dog Toys
- Dog Treats
- Health & Hygiene Products
- Pet Care Items

This helps identify top-performing product segments.

---

### 2. State-Based Branch Distribution
Geographical map displaying branch distribution across different states, allowing regional performance analysis and location-based strategic planning.

---

### 3. Employees in Each Branch
Donut chart representing workforce distribution across 20 branches, enabling comparison of staffing allocation and operational structure.

---

### 4. Year-Based Sales Trend (2019–2025)
Line chart showing annual sales performance, highlighting:
- Growth trends
- Sales fluctuations
- Peak performance years

This supports long-term business planning and revenue forecasting.

---

## Filters & Interactivity

The dashboard includes interactive filters for:
- Category
- State
- Branch ID
- Year range (2019–2025)

These filters allow dynamic analysis and customized data exploration.
